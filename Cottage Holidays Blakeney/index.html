Jollyboat — cosy cottage for two in Blakeney

Privacy Policy

How Cottage Holidays Blakeney looks after your personal information.

What we collect

When you make an enquiry or create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, full UK home address, and your stay details (dates and party size). If you choose to save a passkey, we store the technical credential your device provides — never your fingerprint or face.

Why we collect it & our lawful basis

We use your details to respond to your enquiry, manage your booking, contact you about your stay, and keep records required for our business and tax purposes. Our lawful bases are performance of a contract (your booking) and our legitimate interests in running the business and preventing fraud. Your home address is collected as a standard requirement for holiday-let bookings (guest identification and our terms).

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. It is handled by our website host and email provider so we can store your booking and send confirmations. If you booked through a platform such as Airbnb or Vrbo, that booking is governed by their own privacy policies. We only disclose information where legally required.

How long we keep it

We keep booking records for as long as needed to run the business and meet UK tax and legal obligations (generally up to 6 years). You can delete your account login at any time from your account page; your historical booking records may be retained as above.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can ask to see the personal data we hold about you, correct it, or request its deletion. You can update your contact details and address, and delete your account, from your account page. For anything else, please get in touch using the contact details on the site.

Contact

If you have any questions about your data, please contact us via the “Call to Discuss” number shown on the booking page. This notice is provided for transparency and may be updated from time to time.
